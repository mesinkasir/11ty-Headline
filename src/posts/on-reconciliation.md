I reckon that reconciliation

is a clever trap word

a trick of the mind

a language technique

now used all the time

a deft move

on the Chess Board of Life

so we’ll fail to see

what the powers that be

want to keep

out of sight

and out of the light

reconciliation has a meaning

not meant for you and me

it’s hidden meaning is kind of deep

it’s designed to keep us asleep

and quell rebellions

so we won’t be such hellions

it’s used to win our submission

to their mission system

of eternal domination

it ought to be spelled:

Wreckinciliation

with a ‘w’ –Steven Newcomb
