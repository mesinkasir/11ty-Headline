Headline is an independent local newsroom covering city government, schools, business, culture and the environment. We publish original reporting — no wire copy, no aggregation — written by journalists who live in the neighborhoods they cover.

## What we believe

We think local news works best when it is close to the ground and free to read. Our reporting is funded by members, not paywalls, and every story we publish is available to everyone.

- **Accuracy first.** We correct errors quickly and visibly.
- **Show our work.** Documents, data and sources are linked whenever possible.
- **Cover the follow-up.** The vote matters, but so does what happens after it.

## The team

Our small staff is led by editor-in-chief Adam DJ Brett and city hall reporter Jamie Rivera, with a rotating bench of freelance contributors. You can find each writer’s stories on their author page.

## Get in touch

Tips, corrections and story ideas are always welcome — the best ones come from readers. Visit our [contact page](/contact/) to reach the newsroom.
